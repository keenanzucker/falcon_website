This is Falcon

Our Project

For our Principles of Engineering final project, we decided to build a unique cable-suspended camera system. The idea is similar to a Skycam which is flown above popular sporting events on a stadium scale. However, a smaller, room-sized version of a cable-suspended camera is a untapped market. Recently, the market for amateur filmmakers has increased dramatically. Drones are becoming popular for outdoor and landscape footage. However, an indoor alternative for smooth and downwards footage is non-existent. Our aim to change that! We welcome you to explore our camera system we proudly call Falcon.



Our Subsystems

Mechanical

We designed and fabricated a mechanical system. We used ClearPath servo motors and a unique spooling system.

Electrical

We used three RN-41 Bluetooth modules and well as three Arduino's to send/recieve commands from our Rasberry Pi master node.

Software

We wrote Arduino C code as well as a Python script to control our motors and flightpaths.

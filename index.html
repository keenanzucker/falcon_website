This is Falcon

Our Project

Falcon is a high precision cable-suspended camera motion control system. It offers the room-spanning motion of a drone-mounted camera, without the inherent danger and noise. Carrying a Ronin gimbal and Panasonic GH4 camera, this mechatronic system allows the user to capture stunning footage.


This is one 'node' of our Falcon cable-suspended system.



Our Subsystems

Mechanical

We designed and fabricated our mechanical system. We used ClearPath servo motors and a unique spooling system.

Electrical

We used three RN-41 Bluetooth modules and well as three Arduino's to send/recieve commands.

Software

We wrote Arduino C code as well as a Python script to control our motors and flightpaths.

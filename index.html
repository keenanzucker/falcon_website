This is Falcon

Our Project

Falcon is a high precision cable-suspended camera motion control system. It offers the room-spanning motion of a drone-mounted camera, without the inherent danger and noise. Carrying a Ronin gimbal and Panasonic GH4 camera, this mechatronic system allows the user to capture stunning footage.



Pictured here is one of three nodes in the Falcon cable-suspended system.



Our Subsystems

Click below for more information

Mechanical

We designed and fabricated our mechanical system. We used ClearPath servo motors and a unique spooling system to achieve smooth camera motion.

Electrical

We used three RN-41 Bluetooth modules, Arduino Uno's and protoshields to communicate wirelessly and recieve commands from the computer.

Software

We wrote Arduino C code to as well as Python scripts to control our motors, generate flightpaths, and connect to the bluetooth modules.
